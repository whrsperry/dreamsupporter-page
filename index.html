Greetings from the President

We are a total consulting group whose strength lies in our experience in the HR field.

The temporary staffing industry is facing a mountain of challenges, including the negative image triggered by the Lehman Shock, repeated legal revisions, and a deteriorating recruiting environment.

In this age of VUCA world (Volatility: change and instability, Uncertainty: uncertainty, Complexity: complexity, Ambiguity: vagueness and imprecision), we can naturally expect changes in the indirect external environment, such as exponential progress in AI and IoT and the arrival of the 100-year life age due to advances in medical technology and preventive medicine, making the situation truly chaotic and difficult to predict the medium-to-long term.

Dream Supporter has specialists in each of these areas, and while we are aware of the issues and trends in the industry as a whole, we are also able to make optimal proposals that are tailored to the circumstances and challenges of each individual company. We hope to contribute to the development of the HR industry and the temporary staffing industry by carefully supporting each client company.
